How It Works

MoodifyAI leverages advanced AI techniques to analyze the emotions in a user's uploaded photo. Once the emotion is detected, our algorithm maps it to a curated playlist of songs that match the user's mood. Whether you're feeling happy, sad, angry, or relaxed, MoodifyAI aims to connect your emotions to the perfect soundtrack.

Our Mission

The primary aim of MoodifyAI is to enhance the user's music experience by making it more personal and meaningful. In a world where music often serves as a therapeutic tool, our goal is to combine technology and creativity to create a seamless and personalized music recommendation system that resonates with users on an emotional level.
